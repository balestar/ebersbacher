Mario Ebersbacher,
Chief Executive Officer

Mario Ebersbacher founded Ebersbacher Group with a singular conviction: that private wealth clients deserve the same level of rigour, intelligence, and discretion once reserved only for the largest institutional investors. Over 18 years in private wealth and investment management, he has built a firm that reflects that belief in every client relationship.

Operating from Worcester in the heart of England, Mario and his team work with high-net-worth individuals, family offices, and institutional partners across twelve European markets. His particular expertise lies in cross-border investment structuring, strategic capital deployment, and long-term portfolio advisory.

"Every client is an individual, not a category. Our job is to understand what truly matters to you — and to build a strategy that honours that."
