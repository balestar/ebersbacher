Before we instruct the release of Tranche 1, we ask that you confirm the details of a secure, designated project account or holding facility — this is the account into which our client's investment tranches will be paid.

The account should have a verified available balance of at least €120,000 (10% of the total commitment) and be ready to receive international wire transfers in EUR. It may be a corporate bank account, a regulated solicitor or notary client account, or a secured escrow or licensed digital payment account held with a recognised financial institution. The key criteria are simple: the account must be secure, accessible, and ready to receive funds immediately upon execution of the Term Sheet.

This is not due diligence scepticism — it is the practical step of confirming where the money will land. Once we have the account details and can see that the facility is live and funded, our client's instruction to release Tranche 1 goes out the same day. Everything is built around making this as fast and frictionless as possible for both sides.
