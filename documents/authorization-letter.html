To Whom It May Concern,

This letter is issued by Ebersbacher Group Ltd. (Company No. 12847653), a private limited company incorporated under the laws of England and Wales with its registered office at Whittington Road, Worcester, WR5 2RG, United Kingdom, for the purpose of formally confirming the authority and representation rights of its Chief Executive Officer in connection with all commercial, financial, and contractual matters.

The Board of Ebersbacher Group Ltd. hereby confirms and declares that Mario Ebersbacher, acting in his capacity as Chief Executive Officer and sole Director of the company, is fully and unconditionally authorized to undertake the following on behalf of Ebersbacher Group Ltd.:

Confirmed Scope of Authority
Execute and sign Letters of Intent, Term Sheets, Memoranda of Understanding, and binding investment agreements on behalf of the company and its clients
Represent Ebersbacher Group Ltd. and its investment clients in all negotiations, correspondence, and formal proceedings related to property acquisition, capital deployment, and strategic investment mandates
Engage legal counsel, notaries, solicitors, and third-party advisors in the name of the company for the purposes of due diligence, escrow arrangements, and transaction completion
Confirm and verify investor mandates, direct investment decisions, and authorize the movement of funds in accordance with client instruction and applicable financial regulations
Correspond with counterparties, government bodies, financial institutions, and professional service providers on all matters related to the company's business activities

This authorization is granted without limitation and remains in full force and effect as of the date of this letter. Ebersbacher Group Ltd. accepts full legal responsibility for all actions undertaken by Mario Ebersbacher within the scope of authority described herein.

Any party receiving this letter may rely upon it as conclusive evidence of the authority of Mario Ebersbacher to bind and represent Ebersbacher Group Ltd. in the matters described above.
