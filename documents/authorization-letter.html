RE: Authorization of Mario Ebersbacher to Represent Ebersbacher Group Ltd. in All Matters Pertaining to Investment Transaction EBG-LOI-2026-0630

To Whom It May Concern,

This letter is issued by Ebersbacher Group Ltd., a private limited company incorporated under the laws of England and Wales (Company No. 12847653), with its registered office at Whittington Road, Worcester, WR5 2RG, United Kingdom, for the purpose of formally confirming the authority and representation rights of its Chief Executive Officer and Director.

The company hereby confirms and declares that Mario Ebersbacher, acting in his capacity as Chief Executive Officer and sole Director of Ebersbacher Group Ltd., is fully and unconditionally authorised to act on behalf of the company in the following capacities:

  1. Execute and sign Letters of Intent, Term Sheets, Heads of Terms, Memoranda of Understanding, and binding investment agreements on behalf of the company and its private investor clients
  2. Represent Ebersbacher Group Ltd. and its clients in all negotiations, correspondence, and formal proceedings relating to property acquisition, capital deployment, and strategic investment mandates
  3. Engage legal counsel, notaries, solicitors, and third-party advisors in the name of the company for the purposes of due diligence, escrow arrangements, and transaction completion
  4. Confirm and verify investor mandates, direct investment decisions, and authorise movement of funds in accordance with client instruction and applicable FCA regulations
  5. Correspond with counterparties, government bodies, financial institutions, and professional service providers on all matters relating to the company's business

This authorisation is granted without limitation as to territory or counterparty and remains in full force and effect as of the date hereof until expressly revoked in writing by the company. Ebersbacher Group Ltd. accepts full legal responsibility for all actions undertaken by Mario Ebersbacher within the scope of authority described herein.

Any party receiving this letter may rely upon it as conclusive evidence of the authority of Mario Ebersbacher to bind and represent Ebersbacher Group Ltd.
